CHRIS BROWN

Early Life

Christopher Maurice Brown(Born May 5,1989)is an American singer,rapper,songwriter,dancer and actor was born and grew up in Tappahannock,Virginia.

Career

  • 2002-2004:Career beginnings Brown started his career at the age of 13 and he was discovered by Hitmission Records. He was later signed to labels like Jive Records,J-Records and warner Bros. Records.
  • 2005-2006:Chris Brown and acting debut On November 29,2005 Brown released the self-titled Chris Brown album which debuted number two on the Billboard200 and end up selling over 2 million copies in the United states.
  • 2007-2008:Exclusive On November 6,2007 Brown released his second studio alblum Exclusive which debuted at number four on the US Billboard200 and several songs were debuted on Billboard1oo as well. He also featured in some movies i.e The O.C,Stomp the Yard, My Super Sweet 16 and Christmas.
  • 2009-2010:Graffiti and mixtapes On December 8,2009 Graffiti was released and it was debuted at number seven on the USBillboard200 and also songs in the album like Crawl ,I can Transform Ya and Deuces were debuted on the Billboard100.
